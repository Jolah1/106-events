Questions people ask

Do my guests need to install anything?

No. Guests get a link that opens in their browser, or they reply to the WhatsApp message. Their pass is a QR code on a web page — no app, no account, no password.

How do guests get in on the day?

Every person carries their own QR code, plus-ones included. Staff scan it at the door — or read out its eight characters when a phone is dead — and the count keeps working even if the venue's internet doesn't.

What about our vendors?

Every event carries its own vendor sheet — caterer, decorator, DJ, aso-ebi — with the price agreed, what's been paid, and what's still owed, in naira to the kobo. Nothing lives in someone's head or a chat thread.
